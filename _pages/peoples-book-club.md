The People’s Book Club comes together to read and discuss books that help us understand our history and the challenges facing working people today.

Previously, the book club read Howard Zinn’s A People’s History of the United States. Our current selection is Robert Paxton’s Anatomy of Fascism. To participate, [fill out our interest form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSceaSAZPgjscl5VlVcOkwFuBIZk-f0fpxFMh2uUR_gOsFlhMQ/viewform?usp=sf_link) or email <{{ site.data.social.email.url }}>. Details about meetings of the People’s Book Club are published online on [our calendar](/calendar/).
